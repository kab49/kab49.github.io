Pluchea odorata

Marsh Fleabane

It is a native, aromatic plant found in moist, often saline, habitats like salt marshes and stream beds. It is a short-lived perennial or annual herb that grows up to 4 feet tall and produces clusters of pinkish-purple flowers from summer to fall. The plant is known for its strong scent, which makes it deer-resistant, and its flowers attract bees and butterflies.
