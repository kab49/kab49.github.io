Mojave Mammals

Cardinalidae

The Mojave Desert is home to a diverse range of mammals, including bighorn sheep, mountain lions, mule deer, and various smaller mammals like squirrels, rabbits, and bats. Some mammals, like the Mohave ground squirrel, are endemic to the region. Others, such as the Amargosa vole, have highly specialized habitat requirements and are endangered.
