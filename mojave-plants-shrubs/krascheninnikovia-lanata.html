Krascheninnikovia lanata

Winterfat

it is a small shrub sending erect stem branches to heights between 0.5–1 metre (1.6–3.3 ft). It produces flat lance-shaped leaves up to 3 centimeters long. The stems and gray foliage are covered in woolly white hairs that age to a reddish color. The woolly hairs start development in the late fall and gradually diminish through the winter season.
