Lupinus leucophyllus

Velvet Lupine

Each palmate leaf is divided into 7 to 11 leaflets up to 3 inches (7 cm) long. The herbage is coated in white woolly fibers and stiff hairs. The inflorescence is dense raceme of many flowers, each around a centimeter long. Flower heads dense on stalk 4â€“8 in. long and stout. Flowers white or lavender, often turning brown with age. The patch on the banner petal yellow or brownish. The pointed sepals and the back of the banner are hairy to woolly in texture.
